Research

In my doctoral research, I specialized in developing interpretable computational approaches within the field of biomedicine. My educational background includes a Ph.D. in Electronic and Information Engineering, during which I focused on advancing computational methods for leveraging biomedical heterogeneous networks. Specifically, I developed graph deep learning and network analysis techniques utilizing biomedical knowledge graphs (KG) to address challenges associated with highdimensional feature spaces but limited task data.
